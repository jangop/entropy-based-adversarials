Briefing

In this study you are shown pairs of images and asked whether two images are different or not. Some of the images will be different and some will not be different.
If you think that a pair of images is different, click on the button "Different". If you think that the two images are not different, click on the button "Not different".

Below, we show you some examples of pairs of different and non-different images. Note that it is not always easy to determine whether two images are different or not.
